WHAT IS HARMONYCON?

HarmonyCon is the ultimate celebration of all things pony! Join us in Dallas from February 13 to 15 for our seventh annual convention weekend, where fans from around the world gather to experience the magic of friendship through music, art, and community. From star-studded guest panels featuring official voice actors and creators to our iconic COLTCHELLA concert event, HarmonyCon offers an unforgettable experience for pony fans, young and old. Whether you're here for the exclusive merchandise, socializing opportunities, or simply to celebrate your love of ponies, HarmonyCon is where the magic happens!
